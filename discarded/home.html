Welcome to TimeOn!

Our scheduling program is designed for Businesses/Restaurants. The program offers two views, Manager and Employee.

  • Manager View: Managers input three types of parameters to keep the business profitable: fixed costs, variable costs, and goals (e.g., schedule to not exceed 25% of variable costs).
  • Employee View: Employees can input their availability.

The program generates the maximum number of hours to put on the schedule to stay on budget and gives suggestions for scheduling based on employees' availability.

Features include:

  • Efficient shift swaps.
  • Access to coworkers' contact information for coordination.
  • Total hours worked displayed for each employee for payroll purposes.
  • Adjustable hourly export for temporary workers or a weekly, biweekly, or bimonthly export.
